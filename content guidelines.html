‘Hi-Five’ Welcome to the InsightYou Content Guidelines. Below factors that make a content suggestion worthy. If you keep these guidelines in mind, then nothing stopping your content to get published.

Go in-depth

Our members are here to learn, members love long-form content that is filled with professional insights and details. Content that only touches the surface of a topic with no deep dive into a new perspective or understanding of a process won’t satisfy members’ thirst for knowledge.

Verify the link

Every suggested link must take Insighter directly to the English language content being suggested. A link leading to a website’s homepage instead of a specific content item won’t be accepted.

Stay on the topic and Just tell

Our professional members are looking to improve their knowledge and avoid content fluff. Community members love actionable advice and fruitful recommendations. If your suggested content is overly sales-oriented and highlights a single brand, then chances are that the content will not be approved.

High quality

If the suggested content includes the poor video quality, audio quality, bad grammar, and difficult to understand, then members aren't going to benefit from it, hence it might not get approved.

Avoid over-gating your content

If accessing of content requires our members to pass through several steps or answer a lot of questions, the suggestion probably won’t be approved.Also, wedon’t sendour members to content that is locked behind a paywall.
